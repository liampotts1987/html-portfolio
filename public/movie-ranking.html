The Best Movies According to Liam

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Aliens

This is my favourite film of all time. There are so many great quotes established from this film that can be referenced almost on a daily basis

Scream

The original slasher which spawned a whole genre and created the original take on spoof movies in the Scary Movie series

Top Gun

Top Gun is one of the films I remember most when growing up. Tom Cruise is also one of my favourite actors of all time.
